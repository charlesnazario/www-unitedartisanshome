About Us

United Artisans was established in 2015 with over 10 years of hands-on experience in the trade. Founded on a commitment to exceptional craftsmanship, our experience in all aspects of remodeling and management is what allows us to deliver exceptional quality work, at a fair price, delivering the most value to our clients. Every job is given the same personal attention. We specialize in custom carpentry and remodeling work including kitchens and bathrooms. We can handle large remodeling jobs as well as small renovation projects. Most importantly, you can be assured that the job will get done right, the first time.

United Artisans not only strives to provide top quality craftsmanship, we aim to provide courteous and professional service. Our business has been solely based on word-of-mouth advertising and referrals from satisfied customers. We believe there is no better way to grow our business than to have a satisfied customer refer us to their friends and family. We have been extremely fortunate to have so many wonderful customers that appreciate the value that we add to their homes.
